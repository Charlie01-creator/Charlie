About

Design that understands the business behind it.

I started out building small sites for friends' side hustles in Kampala, and quickly learned the part nobody tells you: a beautiful website that doesn't convert is just expensive decoration. Every project I take on now starts with the business question, not the design question.

Today I work as an independent web designer, developer and digital solutions consultant — partnering with hotels, tourism operators, schools, NGOs and founders who need a digital presence that actually moves the needle: more bookings, more enrollments, more donors, more sales.

I also produce and interview for a Ugandan youth talk show, which sounds unrelated until you realise it's taught me the same thing good web design requires — listening carefully enough to ask the right question before you build anything.

Support
